Our Story

From A Single Press
To An Industry Leader

Press Stamping Industries was founded in 1985 with a singular mission: to manufacture metal components that enable our customers' most ambitious products. Starting with a single 100-ton press and a team of six, we have grown into a 60,000 sq. ft. precision manufacturing facility with over 150 skilled professionals.

"Our vision is to be the most trusted precision metal stamping partner in South Asia — where engineering excellence meets manufacturing reliability."

Today, Press Stamping Industries serves over 200 clients across 12 industries — from automotive giants to defense contractors — delivering parts that meet the tightest tolerances in the industry.
