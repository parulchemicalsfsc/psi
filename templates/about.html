Our Story

From A Single Press
To An Industry Leader

Established in the year 1979, at Vadodara (Gujarat, India), Press Stamping Industries is known as the most reputed manufacturer and supplier of high performance gamut of sheet metal press components, sheet Metal Fabrication Components, and Heavy Machinery Components.

These products are manufactured and designed with high precision using quality approved raw material and innovative technology in compliance with international quality standards. The offered products are highly appreciated for low maintenance, sturdiness, high performance, optimum efficiency and longer service life.

"Our vision is to be recognized globally as a leader in the manufacturing of high-performance sheet metal and heavy machinery components."

We offer these products in various technical specifications at most economical price range. In addition to this, we offer highly reliable and effective press shop services for Power Press Machine, Shearing Machine, Press brake Machine, Fabrication shop, Machining shop and high precision work.
