Who We Are

A Legacy Of Precision

Established in 1985, Press Stamping has grown from a small workshop into one of India's leading press stamping manufacturers. Our commitment to quality, precision, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred partner for industry leaders across automotive, railways, chemical, and power sectors.

With state-of-the-art machinery, ISO-certified processes, and a team of skilled engineers, we deliver components that meet the most stringent quality standards.
